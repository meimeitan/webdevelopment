Announcements

Open House this Friday

Maple Falls Elementary School will host an open house this September 23. The event is open to the public from 1:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.


Parents can discover what their child has been working on for the past year, as well as get updates about our exciting classes and programs.

Annual Art Exhibit Opens on Thursday

The school's annual art exhibit opens on May 5 and will run until May 30. The show features beautiful paintings, sculptures, and drawings in a variety of media, celebrating the different aspects of creativity


It's an excellent opportunity for residents and parents to see what students have been learning and creating over the school year.

Museum Field Trip Postponed

Due to inclement weather, the 3rd Grade field trip to the Inaro Museum of Contemporary Art has been postponed.


Updates will be posted as soon as classes resume.
